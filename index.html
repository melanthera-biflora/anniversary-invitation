It Is Such A
Strange Love Story...

I’ve had a crush on you since grade 11, but I never imagined that life would bring you back to me five years later. You’re right—we’re different in so many ways. Duong Phung is mature, thoughtful, and calm, working in industry, while Van Trang is energetic, impulsive, and carefree, immersed in research and education. But I don’t love you because you’re careful or mature, or because you work hard and were the star in high school. I love you simply because you’re Duong Phung. I cherish the precious weekends we share together, and I’m so grateful to have you in my life. I waited five years to become your partner, so why wouldn’t I wait another five years for a happy ending?

“I promise to come back to you, just as I once said I would.”.
